Cookies

The XYZ service (xyz.service.gov.cy) uses cookies that are strictly essential for it to function correctly.

Essential cookies

All of the following cookies are deleted 30 minutes after you close your browser.

Name Purpose
AppDataSessionCookie This cookie stores a ‘session ID’ which is used to identify your browser session.
AspNetCore.Antiforgery This cookie makes sure that information sent to the server comes from our form, and not elsewhere.
AspNetCore.Culture This cookie is set when you’ve successfully signed in using CY Login

Using CY Login

When you sign in using CY Login (formerly ‘Ariadni’) to use this service, it will save additional cookies on your device.

How to control cookies

Find out how to control and delete cookies on your device.

You can delete all cookies that are already on your device and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

Review of policy

The cookie policy will be reviewed when there are significant changes to it.
